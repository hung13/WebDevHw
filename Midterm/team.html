Tandon Motorsports Inaugural Team

    NYU Tandon Motorsports has been crafted with the intention of creating a team with the greatest diversity and skill. Our team utilizes every individual major, from Computer Science to Mechanical Engineering, in order to create an organized and dependable team with the intention of creating balance. Our diverse team also has experience working for companies such as SpaceX, Google, and Boeing, allowing for practical experience to be applied to our vehicle.

Founders

Austin Hunt

Founder and Captain of NYU Tandon Motorsports

    Austin Hunt is a Mechanical Engineering major at NYU and over the past year has brought our team to fruition. After nearly six months at SpaceX, Austin returned to NYU with the new skills and attitude necessary to found this Baja SAE team. Since then, Austin has not allowed the team's ambition to be stifled and constantly applies his practical knowledge to maximize this team.

Marcus Cheung

Director of External & Internal Media

    Marcus Cheung is a Mechanical Engineering and Integrated Digital Media major at NYU and an experienced photographer and content creator. Over the past year, Marcus has been a part of the Welcome Week media team and has created his own car show, dubbed ThrottleLab, on YouTube.
